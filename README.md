I'm a **BS Computer Science student at the University of the Philippines Visayas** who likes building things that are useful, playable, and a little more polished each time around.

I work mostly across **software development**, **backend systems**, **mobile apps**, and **game development**. Recent projects have taken me through Flutter apps, Supabase-backed systems, TypeScript/Astro websites, Godot prototypes, and plenty of coursework-driven experiments in C, Java, Python, PHP, and Kotlin.

## Featured Work

### [RoomKeeper](https://github.com/TheAmazingTurtle/RoomKeeper)
A local-first Flutter app for tracking room inventory, food stock, laundry counters, rent, and utilities. Built around practical day-to-day organization with no account system or cloud dependency.

### [Knight's Assistant](https://github.com/TheAmazingTurtle/knight-s-assistant)
A cozy pixel RPG MVP in Godot for a portrait phone viewport. The player upgrades a knight-and-porter duo, sells collected materials, and pushes through stage checkpoints.

### [myCondo](https://github.com/InnovaTxch/myCondo)
A Flutter condominium management app for property managers and residents, using Supabase auth, Postgres data, role-based access control, and realtime updates.

### [Pixel Origins V2](https://github.com/Blue-Shell-Studios/Pixel-Origins-V2)
A 2D top-down action RPG prototype in Godot with movement states, combat, health, gold, quest tracking, and HUD systems.

### [The Big Crunch](https://github.com/Blue-Shell-Studios/The-Big-Crunch)
A Godot top-down space action game where the player destroys asteroids, gathers scrap, grows stronger, and progresses toward larger vessels.

### [UPV Komsai.Org Website](https://github.com/UPV-Komsai-Org/upvkomsaiorg-website)
An Astro-based organization website project for UPV Komsai.Org.
